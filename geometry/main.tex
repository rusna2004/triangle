1.5.4 Find the distance from $\vec{I}$ to $BC$.

Solution : 
We know the value of $\vec I$ is
\begin{align}
I &= \frac{1}{\sqrt{37} + 4 + \sqrt{61}} \myvec{\sqrt{61} - 16 - 3\sqrt{37}\\ -\sqrt{61} + 24 - 5\sqrt{37}}
\end{align}
from the problem 1.5.2 .
\begin{align}
\text{The equation of $BC$ from Problem 1.5.1 is:}\notag \\
\vec{n_2^{\top}}\vec{x}+50=0\\
\text{Let d be the distance between $\vec I$ and $BC$, then}\notag \\
d &= \frac{\left|\vec{n_2^{\top}} \vec I + 50\right|}{||\vec{n_2^{\top}}||} \\[20pt]
d &= \frac{\left|\frac{1}{\sqrt{37} + 4 + \sqrt{61}}\myvec{11,-1} \myvec{\sqrt{61} - 16 - 3\sqrt{37}\\ -\sqrt{61} + 24 - 5\sqrt{37}} + 50\right|}{\sqrt{122}} \notag \\[20pt]
&= \frac{\frac{12\sqrt{61} - 28\sqrt{37} - 200}{\sqrt{37} + 4 + \sqrt{61}} + 50}{\sqrt{122}} \notag\\[20pt]
&= \frac{62\sqrt{61} + 22\sqrt{37}}{\sqrt{122} {(\sqrt{37} + 4 + \sqrt{61}})}\notag \\ [20pt]
&= 3.1272 \notag                                       
\end{align}
